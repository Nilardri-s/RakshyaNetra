Important Information

When you press the SOS button, the following will happen:

  • Your current location will be sent to your emergency contacts
  • A pre-written message will be sent to notify them you may be in danger
  • Your phone will automatically start recording audio (if permitted)

Remember: In case of immediate danger, always try to call local emergency services (like 911) first if possible.
