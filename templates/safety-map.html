About the Safety Map

This map shows reported incidents and safety levels based on community reports. Safe areas are shown in green, moderate risk areas in yellow, and high-risk areas in red.

The data is anonymized and updated regularly. Remember that safety conditions can change, so always stay alert regardless of an area's designation.

To contribute to the map, please report incidents you've witnessed or experienced.
